Enums

22 raylib enums exposed with explicit Iron ordinals matching C constants byte-for-byte.

ENUM-01..22 reference

Every Iron enum ordinal matches its raylib C constant byte-for-byte (ENUM-22). Pass them anywhere raylib expects an int; at the FFI boundary they lower to (int)variant.ordinal.

enum CameraProjection

Category: Camera · raylib: enum CameraProjection

2 projection modes: PERSPECTIVE = 0, ORTHOGRAPHIC = 1.

v2.0.0-alpha codegen note

ironc has a known bug when an enum ordinal appears inside a struct initializer. Use Int32(0) for PERSPECTIVE or Int32(1) for ORTHOGRAPHIC at the Camera3D(...) constructor; byte-identical at the ABI. Deferred to post-alpha codegen fix.

var cam = Camera3D(pos, tgt, up, Float32(45.0), Int32(0))  -- PERSPECTIVE
